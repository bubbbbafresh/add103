A Little About Me

Hi, I’m Damien Rose, and I’m 20 years old. Welcome to my personal project where I share a little about myself and the things I’m passionate about. I’m a car enthusiast, a guitar player, and a fan of the local music scene. This space is all about celebrating the things I love and connecting with others who share similar interests. I’ve always been fascinated by working on cars. Whether it’s tinkering with engines, learning about mechanics, or just admiring classic and modern designs, cars have been a big part of my life. There’s something incredibly satisfying about taking something apart, putting it back together, and seeing it work. When I’m not under the hood of a car, you’ll probably find me playing guitar. I love the creativity and freedom that comes with making music, and punk rock has always been my go-to genre. It’s raw, energetic, and full of emotion—everything I look for in music. Not to mention it's insanely easy, and I’m not all that good of a musician. Speaking of punk, I’m a regular at local shows in my area. There’s nothing like the energy of a live show, and I love supporting local bands and artists. It’s a great way to connect with my community and discover new music. This project is a chance for me to share my journey with cars, music, and the local scene. Whether you’re into working on cars, playing guitar, or checking out local punk shows, I hope you find something here that resonates with you.
