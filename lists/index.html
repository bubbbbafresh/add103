Plant Terms

- Photosynthesis
Process by which plants use sunlight to make food.
- Pollination
Process by which pollen is transferred from the male part of a flower to the female part.
- Germination
Process by which a seed develops into a new plant.
- Fertilization
Process by which a sperm cell and an egg cell combine to form a new plant.
- Transpiration
Process by which water is absorbed by plant roots and released into the atmosphere.
